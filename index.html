  • AI removes manual searching by dynamically analyzing user profiles and history.

Problem: Pricing is adjusted manually for peak times, but lacks AI-driven predictive pricing to optimize for demand surges and empty slots.

Problem: Cancellations leave courts empty, leading to lost revenue and missed opportunities for others.

  • AI automatically suggests available slots to users within the radius who can fill it.
  • Maximizes court usage and improves satisfaction.

Problem: High user drop-off after a few games without targeted engagement strategies.

  • Personalized AI-driven campaigns improve retention significantly.

6. AI-Driven Personalized Marketing

Problem: Generic offers reduce user interest and marketing efficiency.

  • Hyper-targeted campaigns for specific sports, locations, and time preferences.
  • Significantly increases user engagement and booking rates.

7. AI-Powered User Safety and Chat Monitoring

Problem: No real-time detection of abusive or suspicious chat conversations between users.

  • AI-powered NLP (Natural Language Processing) models monitor chats for abusive or suspicious content in real-time.
